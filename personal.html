Personal Information

Family

My twin and I were born July 26, 1992. I have 3 older sisters and an older brother; I also have a handful of nieces and nephews. After doing some research, I found out my father is of Ghanaian descent and my mother is of Nigerian descent from the Yoruba tribe. I was born and raised in Ann Arbor, MI; but I am not a Wolverines fan LOL! A lot of people ask me if my sister and I are identical but no we aren't. I have found out that we are sororal (two girls), fraternal twins. Interesting!!
